# 👗 Smart Outfit Suggestion - User Guide

Welcome to **Smart Outfit Suggestion**, your personal AI-powered fashion assistant! This application is designed to take the guesswork out of getting dressed by providing trendy, personalized outfit recommendations tailored to your exact situation.

---

## ✨ Features What Can This App Do?

1. **🎭 Mood-Based Styling:** Your outfit impacts how you feel, and vice versa. Tell the app your current vibe (e.g., Happy, Confident, Cozy), and it will suggest styles that match or elevate your energy.
2. **📅 Occasion Awareness:** Whether you're heading to **College**, the **Office**, a **Party**, or the **Gym**, the AI ensures your look is appropriate, stylish, and comfortable for the event.
3. **🌤 Weather Context:** No more freezing in thin fabrics or sweating in heavy layers. By selecting the current outside world (Sunny, Rainy, Cold, etc.), your outfit practically prepares you for whatever the day holds.
4. **🎨 Curated Color Palettes:** Get harmonious color combinations perfectly suited for your outfit's vibe.
5. **🧠 Smart Stylist Notes:** Alongside your requested attire, receive tailored fashion wisdom, styling tricks, and pro-tips directly from your AI stylist.
6. **🖼 Visual Inspiration:** Instantly generates a curated aesthetic image that captures the "vibe" of your suggested look.

---

## 🚀 How to Use the App

Using the Smart Outfit Suggestion app is as simple as 1-2-3!

### Step 1: Tell Us Your Current Vibe 🎭
Select how you are feeling today from the "Current Vibe" section. Are you feeling adventurous? Romantic? Relaxed? Choose the emoji that best represents your state of mind.

### Step 2: Choose Your Occasion 🎯
Where are you heading? Select your destination under "The Occasion." This helps perfectly calibrate the formality and practicality of your outfit.

### Step 3: Check the Weather ☁️
Select what the weather looks like outside under the "Outside World" section.

### Step 4: Generate Your Look ✨
Click the **Generate Look** button! The AI will process your inputs and, in seconds, present you with:
- A specific top & bottom combination.
- The perfect footwear.
- Accents & accessories to tie the look together.
- A beautiful color palette and aesthetic inspiration image.
- A custom "Stylist Note" explaining why this outfit works.

### Step 5: Try Another Look 🔄
Not entirely sold on the first suggestion? No problem! Just hit the **Try Another Look** button, and the AI will craft a brand-new ensemble tailored to the same parameters.

---

## 💡 Daily Style Wisdom

Every day you open the app, keep an eye out for the **Style Wisdom** box at the bottom. This feature provides quick, bite-sized fashion tips and rules-of-thumb to slowly level-up your everyday wardrobe!
